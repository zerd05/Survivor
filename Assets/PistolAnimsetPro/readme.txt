Pistol Animset Pro
--------------------


This is a complete set of motion capture animations, to build a seamless, third person perspective character with pistol movement for your game.

It is compatible with Rifle Animset Pro animations and controller.


It consists of 80 animations from which you can make game mechanics for:

- Walking
- Running
- Shooting
- Getting hit
- Grenade throwing
- Aiming with Additive animations
- Melee attacks
- Platformer jumping


It also contains:
- Skinned model of a modern day soldier (26,536 tris, 13,813 verts)
- Skinned model of a dummy, to preview animations
- Simple mesh of a pistol, a placeholder

--------------------

Animations are cut to ready-to-use clips. They have an animated Root bone, so you can use Root Motion and move the character around just with animations. All the transitions between standing and walking are included. Animations use Unity's Humanoid rig, but they can be easly converted to Generic or Legacy rig. If you wish to have all those animations in place (with no Root Motion), just uncheck the Enable Root Motion flag on your character prefab or delete the animation from Root bone.

The skeleton that animations are baked on to is a standard, Motionbuilder compatible skeleton. It has Motionbuilder hierarchy and naming convention. Hips, Neck and Head have Z axes pointing forward, so you can use Look At constraints etc.
